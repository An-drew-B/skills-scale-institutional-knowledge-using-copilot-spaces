# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Facilitates agile ceremonies, coaches the team on process adoption, tracks impediments, and fosters team self-management.

### Responsibilities
- Organize and facilitate standups, sprint planning, reviews, and retrospectives
- Coach the team in agile best practices
- Identify, track, and help resolve impediments and blockers
- Foster continuous improvement and psychological safety

### Goals
- Enable effective, self-managing teams
- Bridge communication between technical and non-technical roles
- Maximize team collaboration and velocity

### Typical Communication
- Works closely with Project Manager, Product Manager, and Developers
- Communicates blockers and process feedback in standups and retrospectives

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
Ensures solutions are user-centric; conducts research and prototyping, validates usability.

### Responsibilities
- Collaborate with Product Manager on requirements and research
- Develop wireframes, prototypes, and user journey maps
- Conduct user testing and incorporate feedback
- Document and communicate design specs

### Goals
- Deliver solutions that prioritize user experience and accessibility
- Validate usability prior to development
- Align design work with business outcomes

### Typical Communication
- Iterates with Product Manager and Developers
- Shares design artifacts and communicates feedback in project planning and demos

---

## Technical Writer

### Role Summary
Owns creation and maintenance of documentation; ensures clarity and completeness for users and teams.

### Responsibilities
- Author and update user guides, process documentation, and API references
- Work with all roles for accurate and up-to-date information
- Ensure onboarding materials are up to date
   
### Goals
- Make documentation clear, helpful, and comprehensive
- Support onboarding and ongoing knowledge sharing

### Typical Communication
- Collaborates across all roles for input/review
- Publishes and maintains docs in project repositories

---

## QA Automation Engineer

### Role Summary
Builds and maintains test infrastructure; creates automated test suites, supports release quality.

### Responsibilities
- Develop and maintain automated tests and QA pipelines
- Collaborate with Developers to identify areas for coverage
- Assist manual QA and project teams in validating new features

### Goals
- Ensure release stability and reduce manual regression testing
- Increase automated test coverage

### Typical Communication
- Communicates test results and issues to Developers and Project Manager
- Collaborates with QA/Testing and release participants

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Gathers and refines requirements, models business processes, and helps teams translate goals into actionable work.

### Responsibilities
- Elicit and document requirements through stakeholder conversations
- Model and analyze business goals, processes, and metrics
- Work with Product and Project Managers to clarify scope and priorities

### Goals
- Prevent gaps in requirements or scope
- Link business outcomes to technical deliverables

### Typical Communication
- Facilitates stakeholder interviews and requirements workshops
- Aligns scope with Product Manager and Project Manager
- Shares process improvements and analysis
